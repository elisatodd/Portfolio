Stylized Dust Explosion

Real-time stylized explosion created for my master's in Technical Art & VFX, using Unreal Engine 5 and Niagara. I focused on making the effect feel dynamic and impactful, with smooth timing and well-defined shapes to keep it readable in motion. The explosion features custom sprite-based smoke, sparks, debris, and a satisfying shockwave.

I used this explosion for a small super-hero themed cinematic alongside some other effects.
