About Me

Hi! My name is Eli. I'm a Technical Artist and Programmer from Spain with a background in computer graphics, real-time rendering, and tools development. I took my university degree in Video Game Development in the Complutense University of Madrid and my masters degree on Technical Art and VFX in Voxel School.

During my university years, I participated in multiple projects and internships, collaborating with artists and designers to create efficient, scalable solutions. I'm passionate about graphics pipelines, automation, and simulation techniques, whether in game engines or VFX production environments.
