Procedural Road Generator

I made a fully procedural PCG tool for generating roads in Unreal Engine 5. I wanted to achieve a tool that could modify and cut through any landscape, including mountains, to make road placing for an environment or game map an easy procedure.

I used landscape modifiers and splines to carve paths, spline meshes to adds guardrails and support columns where needed, and I also adapted the landscape material to reveal interior rock on vertical cuts. To finish it all up and add a richer look to the environment, I added a foliage PCG to populate the scene with trees and plants along the spawned road.

This project helped me learn a lot about Unreals PCGs and procedurally generated content in general!
