Lilith: Rise of the Fallen

Lilith: Rise of the Fallen is a survivor-like game with rogue-lite elements that combines combat against hordes of enemies with taking control of the map, affecting both gameplay and visuals of the game. The constant presence of different abilities, enemies, and their interaction with the scenery and the user make the game heavily rely on technical art related tasks.

The main gameplay mechanic in Lilith consists on corrupting the map, painting the environment dynamically in a Splatoon-like way. To make this come to life, we used Render Targets alongside other technical art techniques which you can check out on this page entry {insert link}.
