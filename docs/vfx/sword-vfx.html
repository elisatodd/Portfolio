Magic Sword Charge Effect

This is a real-time VFX I created for my master's degree in Technical Art & VFX. It was made in Unreal Engine 5, using Niagara and custom materials to simulate a sword charging up with energy.

This effect was inspired by Blasphemous by The Game Kitchen, one of my favorite video games. I wanted to capture their dark and powerful energy, translating it into a completely different art style.
